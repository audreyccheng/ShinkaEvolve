<DESCRIPTION>
Improvements to the rate repair logic (Pass 2) incorporating:
1. Physical Constraint Asymmetry: Enforces strict thresholds for "phantom packets" (RX > TX) and loose thresholds for packet loss (TX > RX).
2. Physical Constraint Scoring: Penalizes candidates that imply physically impossible states (RX > TX) during flow conservation solving.
3. Sigmoid Confidence Decay: Uses a non-linear function `1 / (1 + 10*err)` for confidence scoring to better distinguish between noise and gross errors.
4. Fallback Logic: Uses physical constraints to break ties when topology information is missing.
</DESCRIPTION>
