1.  **Beam Search**: Reinstates a continuous quadratic efficiency bonus which granularly rewards partial parallelism, not just perfect fits.
2.  **Adaptive Ruin**: Introduces a dynamic ruin intensity that scales up when the search stagnates, and adds a 'Random Scatter' mode to break dependency chains.
3.  **Enhanced Polish (Gap Repair)**:
    *   **Convergence**: Increases `MAX_PASSES` to allow the repair process to converge.
    *   **LPT Ordering**: Processes transactions in Longest Processing Time order ('Big Rocks First') to settle major structural elements before filling gaps.
    *   **Early Exit**: Optimizes the inner scan loop by stopping immediately if a 'zero-cost' insertion position is found, significantly speeding up the process.
