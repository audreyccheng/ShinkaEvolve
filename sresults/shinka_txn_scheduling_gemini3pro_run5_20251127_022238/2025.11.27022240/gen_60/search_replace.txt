1.  **Beam Search Scoring**: Relax the threshold for the 'Efficiency Bonus' from 0.4 to 0.05 to reward even partial parallelism more aggressively. Re-introduce a distinct 'Perfect Fit Bonus' for zero-cost insertions, which was successful in previous high-performing generations (Gen 35).
2.  **Refinement Phase (Polish)**: Remove the `stride` and `MAX_EVALS` limits in the Local Search. These constraints (evaluating only ~400 moves) severely handicap the algorithm's ability to find local optima. Revert to a full "Gap Repair" scan that runs conditionally (if the candidate is within 5% of the best so far or with 20% probability). This concentrates computational resources on refining high-quality candidates rather than shallowly searching many.
3.  **Gamma Tuning**: Adjust Gamma decay to range from 1.6 to 0.8 to shift balance slightly towards packing in the final stages.
