<DESCRIPTION>
1.  **Beam Search**: Introduced a dynamic scaling factor for the efficiency bonus. The weight decays from 4.0 to 1.0 as the schedule builds. This encourages establishing a highly parallel "skeleton" early on (high reward for hiding latency), while allowing tighter, potentially less parallel packing at the end to close gaps.
2.  **Polish Phase**: Switched back to a pure LPT-ordered scan (Big Rocks First) instead of alternating orders. Increased `MAX_PASSES` significantly (from ~5 to 20) to allow full convergence, relying on the 'Early Exit' optimization (breaking on zero marginal cost) to maintain performance. This ensures the schedule is tightly packed around the large items.
3.  **Ruin Strategy**: Re-introduced the 'Disjoint Blocks' ruin mode to provide better global shuffling, preventing the highly convergent polish from getting stuck in local optima.
