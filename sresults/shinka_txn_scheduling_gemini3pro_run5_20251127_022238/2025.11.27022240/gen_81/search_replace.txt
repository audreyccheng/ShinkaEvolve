1.  **Continuous Efficiency Bonus (Constructive Phase):** Replaces the discrete "Tiered Efficiency" with a continuous quadratic bonus (`3.0 * efficiency^2`). This provides a smoother gradient for the beam search, rewarding partial parallelism (e.g., hiding 30% of latency) which the tiered system (requiring >50%) might miss. This logic proved effective in prior generations (Gen 62).
2.  **Multi-Mode Ruin with Scatter (ILS Phase):** Re-introduces the "Scatter" ruin mode (random removal of non-contiguous transactions). This mode is effective at breaking dependency chains that block-based removal methods (Tail, Block, Disjoint) often miss.
3.  **Convergent Polish (Refinement Phase):** Significantly increases `MAX_PASSES` (from 3-5 to 10-15) in the polish phase. By coupling this with the "Early Exit" optimization (which aborts a scan if a zero-cost insertion is found), the algorithm can afford deeper convergence checks. This allows the deterministic "Big Rocks First" (LPT-ordered) gap repair to fully settle the schedule structure into a high-quality local minimum.
