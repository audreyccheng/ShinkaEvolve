<DESCRIPTION>
1.  **Quartic-Weighted Sampling**: Updated the random sampling in beam search to use `d^4` weights (Quartic) instead of linear or cubic. This aggressively biases exploration towards "heavy" transactions (long duration/cost) early in the schedule, which is critical for minimizing makespan (packing big rocks first).
2.  **Multi-Target Lookahead**: Improved the lookahead mechanism to evaluate the top-3 largest remaining transactions (LPT) instead of just the single largest. This provides a better estimation of the "goodness" of a state by checking if *any* critical transaction fits well next, rather than just one.
3.  **Ruin-and-Recreate ILS**: replaced the simple swap-based perturbation with a stronger "Ruin and Recreate" strategy. This involves removing a random cluster of transactions and re-inserting them greedily using a best-fit heuristic. This allows the algorithm to escape deep local optima by reshaping significant portions of the schedule, which simple swaps cannot easily achieve.
4.  **Parameter Tuning**: Increased `BEAM_WIDTH` slightly and adjusted `SAMPLES_PER_NODE` and `MAX_CHILDREN` to support the more aggressive search strategies.
