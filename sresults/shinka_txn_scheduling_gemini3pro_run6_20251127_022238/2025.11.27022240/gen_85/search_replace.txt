<DESCRIPTION>
Implements a "Quad-Anchor" Beam Search which increases the number of deterministic greedy selections (anchors) from 2 to 4 to better stabilize the critical path construction, and slightly tunes the lookahead metric (0.7 min / 0.3 avg) to prioritize best-fit compatibility.
Simultaneously, simplifes and intensifies the ILS phase by removing the "Chain Ruin" strategy (which often just shifts problems) and focusing on "Aggressive Scattered Ruin" (removing 6-10 random items) and "Block Moves". This combination provides a stable initial schedule followed by high-variance local search to escape optima.
