<DESCRIPTION>
1.  **Enhanced LAHC**:
    -   Added **Stagnation Kick**: Randomly shuffles a segment if no improvement is found for 600 iterations. This helps escape deep local optima during the long Marathon phase.
    -   Added **Cooling Phase**: The last 20% of iterations enforce strict Hill Climbing (no history acceptance) to ensure the final schedule settles at the true bottom of the basin.
    -   **Multi-Scale Block Moves**: Split block moves into Micro (2-4, frequent) for local adjustments and Macro (5-10, rare) for structural changes.
    -   Optimized mutation probabilities.
2.  **Structured Greedy Diversity**:
    -   Ensured the first candidate always uses proven baseline parameters (`alpha=0.05`, `threshold=0.90`) to guarantee at least one strong starting point.
    -   Subsequent candidates use randomized parameters for exploration.
3.  **Iteration Budget Rebalancing**:
    -   Reduced Warmup (150->100) and Sprint (400->250) iterations.
    -   Increased Marathon iterations (3500->4000) to leverage the new Kick and Cooling mechanisms.
