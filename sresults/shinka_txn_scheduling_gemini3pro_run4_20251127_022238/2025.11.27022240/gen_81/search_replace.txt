<DESCRIPTION>
Optimized `get_best_schedule` by integrating advanced meta-heuristic features into the `run_lahc` function and the greedy construction:
1.  **Cooling Phase**: Modified `run_lahc` to switch to strict Hill Climbing for the final 20% of iterations. This ensures the algorithm converges to the bottom of the local basin instead of floating in a suboptimal state allowed by the LAHC history buffer.
2.  **Stagnation Kick**: Added a mechanism to detect stagnation (no improvement for 600 iterations) and trigger a disruptive "kick" (shuffling a sizable segment of the schedule). This helps escape deep local optima during the Marathon phase.
3.  **Multi-Scale Block Mutations**: Refined mutation operators to distinguish between "Micro-Block" shifts (size 2-4, 25% prob) for local dependency optimization and "Macro-Block" shifts (size 5-10, 6% prob) for larger structural reorganization.
4.  **Decaying Alpha Greedy**: Updated the greedy construction heuristic to use a linearly decaying `alpha` weight. It starts high to prioritize placing "Big Rocks" (long transactions) early in the schedule and decays to prioritize tight packing (low makespan cost) later.

    Get optimal schedule using Diverse Greedy with Decay and Multi-Scale LAHC with Cooling.

    Algorithm:
    1.  **Txn Analysis**: Pre-compute transaction operation counts.
    2.  **Diverse Greedy Phase**:
        -   Generate `num_seqs` candidates.
        -   **Decaying Alpha**: Weight for transaction length starts high and decays, prioritizing
            large transactions ("Big Rocks") early and tight fits later.
        -   **Adaptive Construction**: Select from pool of large and random transactions.
        -   **Warmup**: Short LAHC.
    3.  **Sprint Phase**:
        -   Run LAHC on top 3 candidates.
    4.  **Marathon Phase**:
        -   Run extended LAHC on best candidate.
        -   **Advanced LAHC**:
            -   **Multi-Scale Mutation**: Micro-blocks (local) and Macro-blocks (structural).
            -   **Cooling**: Strict hill climbing in final 20% of iterations.
            -   **Stagnation Kick**: Disruptive shuffle if stuck in local optimum.
    """

            txn_lens[i] = 1

                    seg_len = random.randint(8, 16)
                    idx = random.randint(0, slen - seg_len)

                    random.shuffle(segment)
                    curr_s[idx:idx+seg_len] = segment

                    curr_c = workload.get_opt_seq_cost(curr_s)
                    history = [curr_c] * history_len # Reset history
                    last_imp_iter = i
