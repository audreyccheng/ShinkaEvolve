<DESCRIPTION>
Implements a Multi-Candidate Refinement strategy. Instead of refining only the single best greedy schedule, we select the top 3 distinct schedules found during the greedy phase. We perform a preliminary local search (300 iterations) on each to determine which one has the best optimization potential. The winner then undergoes a deep optimization phase (1500 iterations). This helps avoid getting trapped in local optima determined by the initial greedy choice. Additionally, adjusted the local search move probabilities to 70% Insert / 30% Swap (based on prior best performance) and increased "Big Rocks" count to 5.
