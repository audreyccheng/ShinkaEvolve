<DESCRIPTION>
Refines the LAHC local search with three improvements:
1. Multi-Scale Block Mutations: Splits the generic block insert into "Micro" (size 2-4, 30% prob) for local adjustments and "Macro" (size 5-10, 10% prob) for structural changes.
2. Late-Stage Cooling: Enforces pure Hill Climbing in the last 20% of the iteration budget to ensure convergence to the bottom of the basin of attraction, ignoring the LAHC history buffer.
3. Enhanced Kick: Replaces the shuffle-based kick with a "Block Move" kick (size 10-20) to better preserve local structures while escaping stagnation.
