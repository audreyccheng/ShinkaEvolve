<DESCRIPTION>
This edit introduces two major improvements:
1.  **Diversity-Driven Greedy Parameters**: Instead of a fixed alpha and threshold for the "Big Rocks" greedy heuristic, we now randomize `alpha` (0.0 to 0.6) and the Big Rock `threshold_ratio` (0.80 to 0.98) for each sequence generation. This increases structural diversity in the candidate pool.
2.  **Funnel Refinement & Enhanced LAHC**:
    -   Replaces the simple Sprint/Marathon strategy with a 3-stage **Funnel Strategy**: Filter (50 iterations on top 5), Sprint (300 iterations on top 3), and Marathon (2000 iterations on winner). This avoids discarding potentially good candidates too early.
    -   Modifies the LAHC operator probabilities to favor **Block Inserts** (40%) and increases the block size range to `2-8` items to better handle dependency chains.
    -   Adds a **Stagnation Kick** (shuffling a segment) in the Marathon phase if no improvement is found for 600 iterations, helping to escape deep local optima.
