1.  **Randomized Greedy Parameters**: Instead of using fixed values for `BIG_ROCK_THRESHOLD` (0.85) and `WEIGHT_ALPHA` (0.05), this edit randomly samples these parameters for each greedy sequence generation (`alpha` in 0.02-0.08, `threshold` in 0.80-0.95). This enhances the diversity of the initial population, creating schedules with different structural properties for the subsequent optimization phases.
2.  **Enhanced LAHC Operators**: Increases the maximum block size for the Block Shift operator from 4 to 6 and increases its selection probability from 30% to 40%. This allows the algorithm to move larger dependency chains intact, which is crucial for escaping local optima in constrained scheduling problems.
3.  **Increased Iterations**: Bumps Sprint iterations to 250 and Marathon iterations to 2500 to allow deeper convergence.
