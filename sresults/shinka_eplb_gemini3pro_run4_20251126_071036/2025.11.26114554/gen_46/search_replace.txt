<DESCRIPTION>
Refactor `balanced_packing` to include an "Optimal Two-Bin Re-partitioning" (Reshuffle) step in the refinement phase. This step pools items from the heaviest and lightest packs and redistributes them using a greedy heuristic to minimize the difference, escaping local optima that single swaps cannot. Also refines the initialization strategies and refinement loop order for better efficiency.
